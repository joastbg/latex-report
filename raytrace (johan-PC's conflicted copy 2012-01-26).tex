\section{Reflection}
The reflection direction can be found using the surface normal $n$ and the ray direction $d$:\\
\[
\mathbf r = \mathbf d - 2(\mathbf n \cdot \mathbf d ) \mathbf n.
\]
The reflected ray has equation:
\[
\mathbf x = \mathbf y  + u \mathbf r.
\]
